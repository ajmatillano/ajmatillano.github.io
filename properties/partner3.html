What We Offer

Accredited agents will receive a generous sales commission plus commons shares of our company.
Clients who purchase our properties will also receive their units plus common shares of our company.
This offer is limited only to the 1st. 100 units of each of our projects.

  1. Sales Commission

    5% x Total Purchase Price of the Unit

    Example:
    Purchase Price of the Unit = US$100,000
    Sales Commission per Unit = US$100,000 x 5% = US$5,000

  2. Common Shares

    15% x Total Purchase Price of the Unit

    Example:
    Purchase Price of the Unit = US$100,000
    Common Shares per Unit = US$100,000 x 15% = US$15,000


    Share Value
    All shares are based on a par value of US$0.0001 per share and sale value of US$1.00 per share.
    Share prices may increase and fluctuate.
