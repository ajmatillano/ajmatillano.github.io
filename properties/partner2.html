ACASYS Capital Inc. provides investment and consultancy services for companies seeking to raise funds through public listing.

What You Do

Your role is to introduce companies owners seeking to raise funds for their company through public listing.
These companies should have a minimum annual sales of over US$6 million, strong management and growth potential.
Full info of ACASYS Consultancy services can be found here.

What We Offer

  1. Consultancy Fees 1

    5% x Monthly Consultancy Fees received by us.
    The said fees shall cover all of your expenses (airfares, transport, accommodation etc.)
    Example: US$60,000 Consultancy Fees x 5% x 12 months  = US$36,000

  2. Consultancy Fees 2

    2% x Total Shares received by us.
    Example: 100,000,000 Shares x 2% = 2,000,000 shares
    Shares will be transferred to you after the listing of the clients' company on the OTC Markets.

    Share Value
    All shares are based on a par value of US$0.0001 per share and sale value of US$1.00 per share.
    Share prices may increase and fluctuate.
