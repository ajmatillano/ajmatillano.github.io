PRIVACY

Your privacy is important to ACASYS. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that ACASYS collects, and the ways in which ACASYS uses that personal information.

Personal Information Collection

ACASYS may collect and use the following kinds of personal information:

  • Information about your use of this website

  • Information about transactions carried out over this website

  • Information that you provide for the purpose of securing our services.

Using Personal Information

ACASYS may use your personal information to:

  • Administer this website

  • Collect payments from you

  • Send to you statements and invoices

  • Send to you marketing communications

  • Send to you products that you purchase

  • Supply to you services that you purchase

  • Publish information about you on the website

  • Enable your access to and use of the website service

Where ACASYS discloses your personal information to its agents or sub-contractors for these purposes, the agent or sub-contractor in question will be obligated to use that personal information in accordance with the terms of this privacy statement.

In addition to the disclosures reasonably necessary for the purposes identified elsewhere above, ACASYS may disclose your personal information to the extent that it is required to do so by law, in connection with any legal proceedings or prospective legal proceedings, and in order to establish, exercise or defend its legal rights.

Securing Your Data

ACASYS will take reasonable technical and organizational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse or alteration of your personal information.

ACASYS will store all the personal information you provide on its secure servers. Information relating to electronic transactions entered into via this website will be protected by encryption technology.

Cross-Border Data Transfers

Information that ACASYS collects may be stored and processed in and transferred between any of the countries in which ACASYS operates to enable the use of the information in accordance with this privacy policy.

In addition, personal information that you submit for publication on the website will be published on the internet and may be available around the world. You agree to such cross-border transfers of personal information.

Updating This Statement

ACASYS may update this privacy policy by posting a new version on this website.

You should check this page occasionally to ensure you are familiar with any changes.
