  3. III. Purchase Process for ACASYS Property Units

    1. How do I make a reservation? What are the reservation requirements?
      For Pre-Selling Projects (1) accomplish the Letter of Intent (LOI) + Reservation Agreement.
      Attach the Reservation Fee (RF) in Check/Cash amounting to P25,000.00
      All checks are payable to "ACASYSKBAR DEVELOPMENT CORP"

    2. Can I hold the unit once reserved? For how long?
      For local buyers, we can hold the unit for up to 3 days and International buyers for up to 7 days.
      You can notify our office through email.

    3. Is the reservation refundable?
      No. Failure to submit the Down Payment and required documents are also grounds for the forfeiture of Reservation Fee.

    4. How much is the discount if I pay the units in Spot Cash?
      It varies with our projects.

    5. How do I settle the down payment and / or balance of the unit/property?
      It depends on the payment terms.
      The client has to submit the PDC of the equity along with the documentary requirements within 30 days.
      Bank loan application should be done right after the RF has been paid to facilitate loan application easier.

    6. When do I receive my Condominium Certificate Title?
      For Bank Financing - upon full payment of the loan, the bank usually releases the title.
      If and when the client gets disapproved and opted for in-house, the developer will transfer the title, after the loan and all other charges has been paid by the client.

    7. What is a Share Investment?
      A share investment is investing into the stock market by either buying or selling shares. In our case, we issue shares to our property buyers in a given period of time.

    8. What do I get when I buy a property from ACASYS Development?

      1. Sales and Leasing Services of your property
      2. Capital Appreciation of your property
      3. Annual Rental Yield of your property
      4. Annual Company Shares
      5. Your Property Units

    9. When do I receive the company shares?
      You will receive a certificate of shares upon completion of full payment of the property.

    10. What is the resale or rental potential of the development?
      We have various properties from condominium, town houses, and commercial establishments which have different resale and rental potential.
