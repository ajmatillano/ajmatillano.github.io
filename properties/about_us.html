ACASYS Development

ACASYS Development Inc. is registered in the Philippines.
ACASYS Development Inc. is involved in the development of commercial and residential properties in the Philippines and Malaysia for over a decade.

ACASYS has developed the LGS (Light Gauge Steel Frames and Light Weight Concrete) technologies. These technologies has enabled ACASYS to complete its projects at a shorter time frame, higher quality and reducing the construction cost.

ACASYS’ LGS technologies has been recognized and accredited by the Housing and Urban Development Coordinating Council (HUDC) in the Philippines as an “Innovative Technologies for Housing”. The LGS technologies are particularly relevant as ACASYS plans to develop affordable housings to help address the housing backlogs in the Philippines.

ACASYS has done many major projects in Malaysia including iconic projects such as The Haven in Ipoh, The ICON City in Petaling Jaya, The Forresta in Damansara, the state-of-the-art Proton Centre of Excellence in Shah Alam; Segi College in USJ Subang Jaya,Ipoh Specialist Centre in Ipoh, Carrefour Hypermarket in Subang Jaya and Wangsa Maju, Giant Hypermarkets in Bukit Tinggi Kelang and Kelana Jaya, the Mahkota Shopping Mall in Ipoh, Convocation and Recital Hall for the University Malaysia Sabah and various retail/commercial, industrial, banking, financial, institutions, hospitality and resort projects across Malaysia.

ACASYS and its partners has also completed many major projects in the Philippines such as projects for the Ayala Land such as The Lerato Condo in Makati, Uptown Place Tower 1 in Bonafacio Global City, Avida Towers Asten and 2, Towers San Lorenzo, Avida Towers Sucat , Avida Towers Alabang, Grand Monaco Hills in Antipolo Rizal, Grand Monaco Bellevue 1, Grand Monaco Bellevue 2.
